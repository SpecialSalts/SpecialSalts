Our Story & Market Launch

From homeschool project to first market

Special Salts began as a homeschool business project created by Tyler, a young local entrepreneur from Byford.

Tyler first started making bath salt blends with his brothers, creating small batches for family, friends, and local supporters. As the idea grew, he began learning the real skills behind a small business: choosing ingredients, testing blends, designing labels, pricing products, packing orders, setting up market displays, and speaking with customers.

As a young athlete, Tyler was especially interested in products that could be used after sport, training, busy days, or as thoughtful gifts.

Launching at his first market gave Tyler the confidence to keep growing Special Salts, learning through experience, and sharing his products with the local community.

First market complete.

Thank you to everyone who encouraged Tyler, visited the stall, shared the page, or supported his first launch.
