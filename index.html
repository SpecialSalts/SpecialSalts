Crafted by Tyler

Special Salts began as a homeschool project created by Tyler.

At just 12 years old, Tyler first began making bath salts with his brothers in 2022 as part of their homeschool journey.

What started as small Christmas gift batches for family and friends has grown into a young local business, with Special Salts now appearing at community markets and local stockists.

Each blend is handcrafted using simple ingredients, carefully selected essential oils, and a passion for creating beautiful bath and body products people genuinely enjoy using.

Handcrafted with care through our homeschool journey.

Our Blends

Two thoughtfully developed blends, each with its own distinct feel and fragrance profile.

Recovery Blend

Soft floral relaxation

A calming floral blend featuring lavender, geranium, and ylang ylang.

This gentle combination creates a soothing bath, foot soak, or scrub experience, perfect for slowing down at the end of a busy day. The soft floral aroma brings a sense of calm and comfort, making it an ideal choice when you want to unwind and enjoy a quiet moment of relaxation.

Crafted with mineral salts and natural oils, Recovery Blend turns an ordinary bath or body care moment into a simple self-care ritual.

Revive Blend

Fresh citrus mint uplift

A bright and refreshing blend featuring lemon myrtle, lime, eucalyptus, and peppermint.

This uplifting combination offers a fresh, clean aroma that feels vibrant and energising. Revive Blend is ideal when you want a more refreshing bath, foot soak, or scrub experience.

Blended with mineral salts and natural oils, Revive Blend adds a crisp botanical touch to your bathing routine.

How To Use

A simple guide for enjoying both product types at home.

Foot Soak

25g

Add to warm water, allow the salts to dissolve, and soak for 10–15 minutes.

Bath Soak

50g

Add to a warm bath, allow the salts to dissolve fully, and soak for 15–20 minutes.

Body Salt Scrub

Use as needed

Massage a small amount onto damp skin using gentle circular motions, then rinse thoroughly with warm water.

Take care when using body scrub in the shower or bath, as oils and salts may make surfaces slippery. For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes. Discontinue use if irritation occurs.
